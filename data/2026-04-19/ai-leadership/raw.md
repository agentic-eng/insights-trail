- IC skepticism: AI generates impressive demos but fails at sustained, complex projects — "unsalvageable after 10-50 changes"

**Key Themes:**
1. Information asymmetry — executives lack hands-on experience with tools
2. Incentive misalignment — leadership benefits from reduced headcount; workers face displacement fears
3. Demo vs. reality gap — AI performs well in controlled settings but struggles with real-world complexity
4. Conflicting economic interests, not capability disagreements, drive the divide
