## Search 19: Context Engineering (Martin Fowler)

- Context categories: reusable prompts (instructions + guidance), context interfaces (tools, MCP servers, skills), workspace files

- Warning: "illusion of control" — context engineering increases probability but cannot guarantee outcomes
