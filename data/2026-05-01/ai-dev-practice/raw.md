### Production / Reliability
- 5% of all LLM call spans report errors in production; 60% caused by exceeded rate limits (Datadog, Feb 2026) — https://www.datadoghq.com/state-of-ai-engineering/
- 69% of companies now use 3+ models — ibid.
- 37% gap between lab benchmark scores and real-world deployment performance — https://www.getmaxim.ai/articles/top-5-ai-evaluation-platforms-in-2026-comprehensive-comparison-for-production-ai-systems/
- 50x cost variation for similar accuracy — ibid.
- Average token usage more than doubled YoY for median customers, quadrupled for power users — https://machinelearningmastery.com/5-production-scaling-challenges-for-agentic-ai-in-2026/
- Framework adoption doubled YoY (LangChain, LangGraph, Pydantic AI, Vercel AI SDK): 9% → 18% — https://www.datadoghq.com/state-of-ai-engineering/
- MMLU and MMLU-Pro functionally saturated above 88% for frontier models — https://kili-technology.com/blog/ai-benchmarks-guide-the-top-evaluations-in-2026-and-why-theyre-not-enough
- LLM hallucination rates: 15-52% range across 37 models; Claude 4.6 Sonnet ~3% (best performer) — https://modelslab.com/blog/llm/llm-hallucination-rates-2026
- 52% of enterprise AI responses contain fabrications on ungoverned data vs. ~0% on governed data — https://atlan.com/know/llm-hallucinations/
- Structured prompts ("cite your sources", "if uncertain, say so") reduce hallucination rates 20-40% — ibid.
- Over 70% of errors in LLM apps from incomplete/irrelevant/poorly structured context — https://www.meta-intelligence.tech/en/insight-context-engineering
- RAG failure: 73% of time failure is in retrieval, not generation — https://roadie.io/blog/rag-vs-context-engineering-production/
- Contextual retrieval (prepending chunk context before embedding) reduces retrieval failures by 49%; up to 67% with reranking — https://blog.supermemory.ai/what-is-context-engineering-complete-guide/
- Atlan 2026: context failures (missing, stale, conflicting) are a leading cause of production AI breakdowns
